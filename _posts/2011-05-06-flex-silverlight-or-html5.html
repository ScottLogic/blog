Building on the experience and expertise within Scott Logic, my colleague, Colin Eberhardt, has produced an excellent whitepaper considering the three big web technologies at the moment: Flex, Silverlight and HTML5.

Recent advances in web technologies have resulted in a complex landscape for applicationdevelopers to navigate. Coupled with the recent boom in new platforms, from desktops, netbooks,smartphones to tablets, making an informed and future proof technology choice is all the moredifficult. In this paper we will set technology bias and politics aside to navigate the similarities anddifferences between Flex, Silverlight and HTML5 and give you the power to decide.

This white-paper is aimed at technical decision makers who are looking to choose the correcttechnology for web application development.
